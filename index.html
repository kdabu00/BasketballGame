Basketball 

         Speed Basketball

Rules:
	Click away from the ball (Only click once at a time!)
	
	Ball direction is opposite where the mouse is
	
	Do trick shots! Bounce off the left side of the canvas for bonus points
	
	Timer starts at 5 points or more! you have 24 seconds to make the next basket(Sorry no buzzer beaters)
	
	Gain more time as you make baskets, trick shots add additional time
	
	If the ball touches the ground or the timer reaches 0: new game
	
	(Some shots can only be made by bouncing)
